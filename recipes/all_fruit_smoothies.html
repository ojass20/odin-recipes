All fruit smoothie

Home

Quick, easy smoothies made entirely with fruit!

Details

  • Prep time : 10 min
  • Additional time : 10 min
  • Total time : 20 min
  • Servings : 2
  • Yield : 2 glasses

Nutritional facts

  • Calories : 205
  • Fat : 1g
  • Carbs : 51g
  • Protein : 2g

Ingredients

  • 1 cup pineapple juice
  • 1 large banana, cut into chunks
  • 1 cup frozen strawberries
  • 1 cup frozen blueberries

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Pour pineapple juice into a blender.
  3. Add banana, strawberries, and blueberries.
  4. Cover and blend until smooth, about 1 minute.
  5. Pour into 2 glasses.

Your recipe is completed and your smoothie is ready. Enjoy this delicious meal and share it with your friends and family.
